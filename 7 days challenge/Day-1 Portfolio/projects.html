Calculator App

Our calculator app is a handy tool for performing basic arithmetic operations such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it features a user-friendly interface that allows users to input numbers and perform calculations with ease. With its responsive design, the calculator app adapts to various screen sizes, making it accessible on both desktop and mobile devices. Whether you're balancing your budget or solving math problems on the go, our calculator app is here to simplify your calculations.

Weather App

Stay ahead of the forecast with our weather app, built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. With real-time weather data from reliable sources, our app provides accurate weather information for any location worldwide. Users can simply enter their city or zip code to view current weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more. The app's intuitive interface displays weather updates in a visually appealing manner, with dynamic icons and animations that reflect the current weather conditions. Whether you're planning a weekend getaway or simply checking the daily forecast, our weather app is your go-to companion for staying informed about the weather.
