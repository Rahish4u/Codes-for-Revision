Our History

Your Restaurant was founded in 2015 by Rahish Ali with a vision to create a culinary experience that combines passion in cooking with offering indian food. Starting as a small family-owned establishment, we quickly gained recognition for our commitment to quality ingredients, exceptional service, and vibrant atmosphere.

Over the years, we've celebrated many milestones, from expanding our menu to opening additional locations. Despite our growth, we remain dedicated to our roots and continue to uphold the values that define us.
